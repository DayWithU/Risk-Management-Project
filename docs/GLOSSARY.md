# Glossary — Full forms and short definitions

RACI — Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed
- Responsible: Person(s) who do the work.
- Accountable: Person who owns the outcome (only one).
- Consulted: People who provide input (two‑way).
- Informed: People who are kept up to date (one‑way).

RAID — Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Decisions
- Risks: Uncertain events that may affect objectives (with mitigation).
- Assumptions: Things the project team accepts as true for planning.
- Issues: Current problems that need resolution (action required).
- Decisions: Formal decisions made (who decided, rationale).

BCP — Business Continuity Plan
- Plan to keep critical business functions running during disruptions.

DR — Disaster Recovery
- Technical plan to restore IT systems after a major failure.

RTO — Recovery Time Objective
- Maximum acceptable time to restore a service after an outage.

RPO — Recovery Point Objective
- Maximum acceptable amount of data loss, measured in time.

KPI — Key Performance Indicator
- Measurable metric used to evaluate success (e.g., % risks closed).

PMO — Project Management Office
- Team or function that standardizes project practices and governance.

SLA — Service Level Agreement
- Contractual commitments about service performance (uptime, response).

Tips
- Keep definitions short and put full forms first (as above) so reviewers immediately understand abbreviations.
